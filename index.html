About Us

Access Livelihoods (AL) is a group of Hyderabad-based social enterprises that work with marginalised communities to reimagine their livelihoods...

Communities

Ninety-two percent of India's labour force, or 63% of the country's GDP, is unorganised. Just 25% of non-farm workers are organised; the majority of the agriculture sector is classified as informal. The value that these communities contribute to is not fairly distributed to them. ALC India focuses on the livelihoods of ten sizable communities in order to spark the development of businesses and jobs, so initiating the process of their transition towards self-sufficiency.

Innovative Platforms that Shape the Livelihoods Environment

Livelihood ecosystems can thrive only in environments that enable their sustenance and growth. We aim to ensure this through our platforms. ALC India is currently engaged in creating platforms that focus on finance of livelihoods, human resources required for the livelihoods sector, knowledge for practitioners in the livelihoods sector and a platform for the communities who are the ultimate stakeholders.
